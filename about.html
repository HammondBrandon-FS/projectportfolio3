About Me

Lindsey Hammond is a licensed real estate agent on the Real Team Experts for Triple R Real Estate. The Real Team Experts serve in the state of Georgia and Alabama serving Carroll, Paulding, Haralson, Heard, Douglas, Polk, Coweta, Randolph, Cleburne, and Cherokee counties plus many more in the state of Georgia and Alabama. Lindsey is a proud military spouse with three children, a 7 year old and a 2 year old, and a new baby girl.
